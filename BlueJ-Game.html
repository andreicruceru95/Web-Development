The Game

The action takes place in a post-apocalyptic world. The player finds itself in a fortress, a place where humans are hiding themselves from the outside evil. While exploring the fortress, the player will find various items and gold(game currency) hidden in bags and barrels.
There are a few soldiers and 'humans' in the fortress. They will communicate with the player, and give him tasks.

Graphics

As we are not yet familiar with graphics rendering, I decided to make the game a bit more fun and print the game on the terminal window, and it looks a lot better than I anticipated.
The map is a 2D Array (Matrix), and the player has a visual field that only alows him to see three squares around him. I used html4 and html5 characters to represend various objects on the map, like monsters and people.
